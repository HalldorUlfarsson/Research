# Design and Specifications for Hallorophone

The Hallorophone is designed to be an innovative musical instrument that combines traditional acoustic elements with modern digital sound processing. Below are its design specifications:

## General Features
- **Type**: Hybrid instrument (acoustic and electronic)
- **Material**: Eco-friendly wood and metal components

## Acoustic Design
- **Body Shape**: Curved, similar to a violin for optimal sound projection
- **Strings**: 6 nylon strings suitable for versatility in sound

## Electronic Components
- **Microcontroller**: ARM Cortex M4 for sound processing
- **Connectivity**: Bluetooth and USB outputs for digital interfacing

## Sound Characteristics
- **Range**: A4 to C8 (440Hz to 4186Hz)
- **Customization**: Different sound modulation options available via controls on the body of the instrument.

## Dimensions
- **Length**: 32 inches
- **Width**: 12 inches
- **Depth**: 4 inches

## Weight
- Approximately 5 lbs.

## Power Supply
- Rechargeable Lithium-Ion battery with a lifespan of up to 10 hours of continuous use.

## Target Market
- Musicians seeking a unique sound blending traditional and digital music.

## Conclusion
The Hallorophone represents a convergence of art and technology, aiming to create a new musical experience for performers and listeners alike.
