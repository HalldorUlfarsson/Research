# Development History of the Hallorophone

## 2026-03-23 09:58:23 UTC
The Hallorophone project began with ideas for creating a new instrument that combines aspects of various sound production technologies.

## Key Milestones
- **Phase 1**: Initial concept and design discussions were held in early 2026, focusing on acoustic properties and user interaction.
- **Phase 2**: Prototyping began, utilizing 3D printing to create early models of the device.
- **Phase 3**: Testing of initial prototypes and gathering of feedback from musicians.

## Future Directions
The project is still ongoing, with plans to refine the design and explore new soundscapes using the Hallorophone.
